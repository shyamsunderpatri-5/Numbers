**Chaldean Numerology – a deeper understanding**
Chaldean Numerology is based on the principles that the Universe is built by vibrations, and that everything is vibrations that vibrate with different frequencies. Every frequency attracts its equal. A balanced vibration attracts other balanced vibrations, and unbalanced vibrations attract other similarly unbalanced vibrations. Chaldean Numerology is also based on the fact that everything is destiny, and that we all have a destiny and that our destiny is strictly unavoidable.

**The Base Vibrations (Single Numbers 1-9)**
- **Numerology number 1**: Represented by the Sun. Dictates the "feminine winner mentality," desiring freedom and abundance. Strong dislikes for authorities; they want to be authorities themselves. Creative and perfectionist.
- **Numerology number 2**: Represented by the Moon. Soft and often weak. Strong creativity, often melancholic. Main strength is reading people and empathy, but may fail to see the big picture.
- **Numerology number 3**: Represented by Jupiter. Masculine winner mentality. Dynamic, ambitious, and focused on growth.
- **Numerology number 4**: Represented by Uranus. Innovative, unique, and often rebellious. Focused on freedom.
- **Numerology number 5**: Represented by Mercury. Social skills, adventure, and communication. Easy-going and living in the moment.
- **Numerology number 6**: Represented by Venus. Harmony, beauty, and love. Balance-seeking.
- **Numerology number 7**: Represented by Neptune. Philosophical, seeking deeper meaning and truth. Esoteric interests.
- **Numerology number 8**: Represented by Saturn. Major destiny number. Serious, disciplined, and often dealing with heavy karma.
- **Numerology number 9**: Represented by Mars. Highest number, divine number. "Everything." Focused on justice, integrity, and honor. Enforcers of Karma.

**Compound Numbers (Excerpts)**

**Master Numbers in Numerology**
The article explains that master numbers are compound numbers where the same digit is repeated (11, 22, 33, 44).
- **Master number 11 (11/2)**: Known for stress and anxiety. High sensitivity but often overwhelming.
- **Master number 22 (22/4)**: Can mean living in a happy fantasy world far beyond reality. Often associated with mental disorders such as mythomania, autism, OCD, or Asperger’s when unbalanced.
- **Master number 33 (33/6)**: Fortunate number. Focuses on materialism, business, due diligence, and high integrity. Promises assistance from people of rank.
- **Master number 44 (44/8)**: Mentioned as having significant potential for disaster due to the combination of double 4 and 8, representing a heavy karmic burden.

The article emphasizes that these numbers carry higher frequencies and therefore higher responsibilities and potential for both great success and significant challenges.
